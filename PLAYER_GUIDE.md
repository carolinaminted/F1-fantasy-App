## 3. Making Your Picks

This is the core gameplay. **Submit before the lock-in time** (usually Qualifying start).

### Your Selection:

| Category | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Class A Teams | Pick **2** (top-tier constructors) |
| Class B Teams | Pick **1** (midfield constructors) |
| Class A Drivers | Pick **3** |
| Class B Drivers | Pick **2** |
| Fastest Lap | Pick **1** driver |

### How to Submit:

1. Tap **Picks** (Steering Wheel icon)
2. Select the **Event** from the dropdown (if not auto-selected)
3. Make your selections for Teams, Drivers, and Fastest Lap
4. Review your choices
5. Tap **Lock In Picks** (big red button)

## â“ FAQ

### Picks & Scoring

**Q: Can I change my picks after Qualifying starts?**
A: No. Once the countdown hits zero, forms are locked to ensure fairness.

**Q: Why isn't my score updating during the race?**
A: Scores are updated by the Admin shortly after the race finishes. Use the Refresh button to check for updates.

**Q: How are points calculated?**
A: Points come from your drivers' finishing positions, team results, qualifying performance, sprint races (when applicable), and fastest lap bonuses.

### Account & Access

**Q: I forgot my password!**
A: On the login screen, tap **"Forgot Password?"** to receive a reset link via email.

**Q: I paid but still see "Pit Lane Closed"**
A: Payments require Admin approval. If it's been more than 24 hours, contact the League Administrator.

**Q: Can I change my Team Name?**
A: Contact the League Administrator â€” this can be updated on the backend.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app isn't loading / showing errors**
A: Try refreshing the page. If issues persist, clear your browser cache or try a different browser.

**Q: I can't see my picks from last race**
A: Go to Profile â†’ scroll to Pick History â†’ tap the race to expand.
